Egg Friend Rice Recipe

Descriptions

egg fried rice is an easy stir fry that anyone can make. But to make it light, fluffy and tasty, you need to follow some traditional tips and tricks.

Ingredients

  • white rice, cooked and chilled
  • eggs
  • light soy sauce
  • sesame oil
  • salt & white pepper
  • neutral cooking oil

Steps

  1. scramble the eggs
  2. stir the rice
  3. combine and season
